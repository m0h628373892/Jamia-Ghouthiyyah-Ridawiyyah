Our Values & Manhaj

Our teachings and methodology are firmly aligned with the doctrine of Ala Hazrat Imam Ahmad Raza Khan and the path of the pious predecessors (Aslaf).


We uphold the beliefs and practices of Ahl al-Sunnah wa’l-Jama‘ah, emphasising correct creed, sound scholarship, spiritual refinement, and service to the Ummah.
